Thank You For Your Donation


How your Food will be collected:



Our Volunteers will reach you at the address given by you at the time of donation and collect food at your door step.

As we are going through a world crisis, we assure you that we are following all the necessary covid guidelines while collecting and delivering the food.



After collecting the food:



Once the food has been collected by the volunteers, it reaches the main center of the concerned city.

Here the food is packed into donation boxes based on the kind of fooditem. We make sure that the food doesn't get spoiled or dirty while packing.

We are following covid protocols while packing the food. We maintain sanitized environmnet.


Food Distribution:



The packed food is distributed to the needy people, orphanges; with atmost care.
